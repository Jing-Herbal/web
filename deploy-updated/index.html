Evidence tiers are updated quarterly. When a meta-analysis changes the picture — even if it weakens the case — the rating changes. We don't hide unflattering results.

Compliance
Ships clean, every time
Every shipment includes a full documentation stack: binomial ingredient declarations, ISO/IEC 17025 COA, GMP certificate, and HS tariff code pre-printed on the outer carton. We've solved the customs problem.
Quality
Standardised extracts only
We sell standardised extracts — not raw herbs. Every product declares its active compound percentage. You know exactly what you're getting, in exactly the form studied in the clinical trials we cite.
Transparency
We publish what we don't sell
Our database includes herbs we've reviewed and decided not to stock — Aconite, Aristolochia, raw He Shou Wu. Those decisions are documented and public. Transparency is not marketing — it's the product.
