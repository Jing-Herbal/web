01

Who we are

This Privacy Policy applies to Jing Pty Ltd (ABN 15 677 842 723), trading as Jing ("we", "us", "our"). We operate the website at jingherbal.com and the online store at shop.jingherbal.com.

We are committed to protecting your personal information and complying with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). This policy explains how we collect, use, hold and disclose your personal information.

02

What personal information we collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information: name and email address when you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us
  • Order information: name, email, shipping address and payment details when you place an order through our Shopify store
  • Communications: any information you provide when you contact us by email or through our website
  • Usage data: information about how you use our website, including pages visited and time spent (collected anonymously via analytics)

We do not collect sensitive information such as health information, financial account details (payment processing is handled by Shopify and Stripe), or government identifiers.

03

How we collect it

We collect personal information in the following ways:

  • Directly from you: when you subscribe to our email newsletter, place an order, or contact us
  • Automatically: when you visit our website, we may collect anonymous usage data through analytics tools
  • Through third-party platforms: when you interact with our social media accounts or Shopify store

We will always tell you why we are collecting your information at or before the time we collect it.

04

Why we collect it

We collect personal information for the following primary purposes:

  • To send you our newsletter and updates about new herb dossiers, protocols and clinical research (only with your consent)
  • To process and fulfil your orders
  • To respond to your enquiries and provide customer support
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with our legal obligations

We will not collect personal information unless it is reasonably necessary for one of these purposes.

05

How we use your information

We use your personal information only for the purpose for which it was collected, or for a directly related secondary purpose. Specifically:

  • Email address: used to send you newsletters and marketing communications you have opted into, and to communicate about your orders
  • Order information: used to process your purchase, arrange delivery, and provide after-sales support
  • Usage data: used in aggregate, anonymised form to understand how people use our website and improve it

We will never use your personal information for a purpose that would be unexpected or that you have not consented to.

06

Disclosure to third parties

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

We may share your personal information with the following third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business:

  • Klaviyo — email marketing platform. Your email address is stored and processed by Klaviyo to send newsletters. Klaviyo's privacy policy is available at klaviyo.com.
  • Shopify — e-commerce platform. Order and payment information is processed and stored by Shopify. Shopify's privacy policy is at shopify.com.
  • Netlify — website hosting provider. Netlify processes basic server logs. Privacy policy at netlify.com.

These providers are only authorised to use your information as necessary to provide services to us. We use contractual and other measures to ensure they protect your information.

We may also disclose your personal information where required by law, court order, or in connection with legal proceedings.

07

Overseas disclosure

Some of our third-party service providers store and process data outside Australia. Klaviyo and Shopify are US-based companies and their servers are located in the United States.

Before disclosing your personal information to an overseas recipient, we take reasonable steps to ensure the recipient complies with the Australian Privacy Principles or is otherwise bound by a law or binding scheme that provides substantially similar protection.

By providing us with your personal information, you consent to us disclosing it to overseas recipients for the purposes described in this policy.

08

Storage and security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorised access, modification or disclosure. These steps include:

  • Using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmitted to and from our website
  • Using reputable, security-audited third-party platforms (Shopify, Klaviyo) to store customer data
  • Limiting access to personal information to staff who need it to perform their role

When we no longer need your personal information, we will take reasonable steps to destroy or de-identify it.

09

Access and correction

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of your personal information if it is inaccurate, out of date, incomplete, or misleading

To make a request, please contact us at hello@jingherbal.com. We will respond within 30 days. We will not charge a fee for making a request, though we may charge a reasonable fee for providing access in complex cases.

In some circumstances we may decline your request, for example where providing access would be unlawful or where it would impact the privacy of other individuals. If we decline, we will tell you why in writing.

10

Cookies and tracking

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to help us understand how visitors use the site.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: required for the website to function correctly. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting information anonymously. We use privacy-first analytics tools that do not identify individual users.
  • Marketing cookies: set by Klaviyo to enable our newsletter sign-up form to function and to identify returning visitors.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

11

Marketing communications

We will only send you marketing communications (such as our newsletter) if you have explicitly opted in. Every marketing email we send includes an unsubscribe link that allows you to opt out at any time.

To unsubscribe, either click the unsubscribe link in any of our emails or contact us at hello@jingherbal.com.

We comply with the Spam Act 2003 (Cth) and will not send unsolicited commercial electronic messages.

Complaints

We will acknowledge your complaint within 5 business days and aim to resolve it within 30 days. If we cannot resolve your complaint to your satisfaction, you may refer it to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC):
